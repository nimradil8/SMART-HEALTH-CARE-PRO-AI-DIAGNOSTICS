🎯 Objective & Industry

Project Objective

  • Build an AI-powered clinical command center for automated medical image diagnosis and triage.
  • Enable real-time lesion detection, classification, and measurement using deep neural networks.
  • Integrate contactless vital monitoring (rPPG) and AI clinical report generation.
  • Ensure HIPAA-compliant data handling with automated OCR-based anonymization.
  • Reduce diagnostic latency and assist clinicians with intelligent batch triage queues.

Industry Domain

🏥

Healthcare Technology

AI-driven diagnostic platforms transforming radiology, dermatology, and pulmonology workflows.

🤖

Med-Tech / AI

Intersection of computer vision, LLMs, and biomedical signal processing for next-gen patient care.

⚙️ Methodology & Working

🧠

Deep Learning Core

EfficientNetV2 architecture trained for 3-class classification: Normal, Medium Risk, and High Risk.

🔥

Grad-CAM Visualization

Layered neural attention maps at Early, Middle, Mid-Deep, and Deep layers for clinical explainability.

📄

AI Report Generation

OpenRouter API integration with Gemini & Llama models for structured clinical PDF reports.

❤️

Contactless Vitals

rPPG signal extraction from facial videos using FFT bandpass filtering for heart & respiration rate.

🛡️

HIPAA Shield

EasyOCR-based automatic text redaction to anonymize patient data before processing.

🚑

Batch Triage

Multi-image upload with severity sorting, emergency queue management, and comparative analytics.
